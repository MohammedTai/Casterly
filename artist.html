OUR TEAM

Mohammed Afif Tai
Founder and CEO

Mohammed A. Tai is a vivid art enthusiast and critic. He founded Casterly alongside his friend and partner in business Jian Ming Lin. His mission was to help empower and uplift underrepresented artists. His wide expertise in marketing would be an essential tool used to help sell the masterpieces of his team of artists. As an entrepreneur, Mohammed created Casterly as a non-profit, for his only goal is to see the talented artists and jewelry crafters of Casterly succeed

Jian Ming Lin
Chief Operating Offier

Jian Ming Lin has always been interested and captivated by art since a very young age. He is not just an art enthusiast but also an art collector that hopes to bring his love of art to help uprising artists have a platform to showcase their art to art collectors around the world. So accompanied by this enthusiasm, Jian and Mohammed A. Tai created Casterly to help uprising artists with this goal in mind. Thus, with his extensive knowledge in digital marketing, he hopes that along with his Casterly team, he can provide uprising artists with a platform to efficiently sell their products

Danny H. Nunez
Professional Artist

Danny H. Nunez has had a hard love for fine art since he was four years old. He taught himself how to draw/paint by practicing every day, and his skills have progressed year by year. He's 23 years old now and has high dreams and aspirations to become a world-renowned/household-known artist. What he loves most about a piece is how much they serve as a stamp to the personality of the artist. He believes that through paint stroke choices from an artist, one can find who they really were as people. He hopes to one day inspire the younger generations of artists to view the world of art in ways much more than the eyes can see. He lives a happy life in New York City with his two beautiful kittens.

Ethel Floon
Professional Artist

Ethel Floon is a multimedia Artist from Louisiana. She Graduated from Memphis College of Art. With a BFA in painting and drawing. Specializing in landscape painting, figures and portraits, and abstraction. Explores color theory, drawing and composition skills, sewing, fabrication/ textiles
